Welcome! I am Haokai Ding, an undergraduate student at the Future Technology School, Shenzhen Technology University, expecting my Bachelor of Engineering degree in July 2026[cite: 4, 6, 7].

My research is centered on the design and application of novel robotic mechanisms and advanced spectroscopy techniques. My recent work includes developing underactuated robotic grippers based on the Peaucellier linkage, which has resulted in three accepted papers at the 2025 IEEE ARM, CASE, and IROS conferences[cite: 11, 13, 15, 27]. I also have experience in utilizing excimer lasers for Laser-Induced Breakdown Spectroscopy (LIBS) and developing automated detection workflows with LabVIEW[cite: 17, 18, 21].
