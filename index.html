About Me

I am an undergraduate student at the Future Technology School of Shenzhen Technology University (2022-2026), concurrently participating in the Tsinghua University Tsien Excellence in Engineering Program at Shenzhen X-Institute as an X Scholar (2023-Present).

My research focuses on novel robotic mechanisms and advanced spectroscopy techniques. Current projects include developing underactuated robotic grippers based on the Peaucellier linkage, and practical applications of excimer lasers for Laser-Induced Breakdown Spectroscopy (LIBS).

Research Interests

  • Novel robotic mechanisms and gripper design
  • Underactuated systems and compliant mechanisms
  • Laser-Induced Breakdown Spectroscopy (LIBS)
  • Advanced manufacturing technologies
