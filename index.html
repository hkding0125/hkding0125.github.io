About Me

I am a third-year undergraduate student at the Future Technology School of Shenzhen Technology University (2022-2026), concurrently participating in the Tsinghua University Tsien Excellence in Engineering Program at Shenzhen X-Institute as an X Scholar (2023-Present).

My research focuses on robotic mechanisms and spectroscopy applications. Current projects include developing underactuated robotic grippers based on the Peaucellier linkage for industrial applications, and practical implementations of excimer lasers for Laser-Induced Breakdown Spectroscopy (LIBS) in environmental monitoring.

Research Interests

  • Novel robotic mechanisms and gripper design
  • Underactuated systems and compliant mechanisms
  • Laser-Induced Breakdown Spectroscopy (LIBS)
  • Advanced manufacturing technologies
  • Robotic applications in spectroscopy
